Our Courses

Nationally recognised first aid and CPR training for individuals and workplaces.

HLTAID011

Provide First Aid

Full first aid course including CPR, fractures, bleeding, burns, shock, asthma, anaphylaxis and more.

Duration: 1 day (with pre-course online theory)

Ideal for: Workplace first aiders, carers, general public.

HLTAID009

Provide CPR

Short annual refresher covering DRSABCD, CPR and AED use on adults, children and infants.

Duration: Approx. 2–3 hours face-to-face

Ideal for: Anyone needing CPR update for work.

HLTAID012

Childcare First Aid

First aid, CPR, asthma and anaphylaxis for educators and support staff in early childhood settings.

Duration: 1 day (blended delivery available)

Ideal for: Childcare, OOSH, family day care, schools.

GROUPS

Workplace & Group Training

On-site training at your workplace or community venue, with flexible times and group discounts.

Min group size: by arrangement

Location: Sydney metro & surrounding areas.

Need help choosing the right course?

If you’re unsure which unit of competency you need for your job or industry, send us a message with your role and any position description or employer requirements. We’re happy to help you select the correct course.
