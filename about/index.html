About Community First Aid & CPR Training

Friendly, practical training that gives you real-world skills, not just a certificate.

Our Story

At Community First Aid & CPR Training, our mission is simple: to empower everyday people with the skills and confidence to save lives. Founded in Sydney, we started with one clear purpose — to make high-quality, practical first aid education accessible, affordable, and genuinely useful for everyone.



With years of experience in healthcare, community services and education, our team saw a gap: many first aid courses were either too complicated, too expensive or not hands-on enough. We wanted to change that.



Today, we are proud to support families, schools, childcare centres, workplaces and community groups across Sydney. Every student we teach becomes part of a safer community — and that is why we do what we do.



At Community First Aid & CPR Training, your confidence is our goal. We are here to help you be ready, stay calm and save lives when it matters most.
