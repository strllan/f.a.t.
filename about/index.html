About Sydney First Aid & CPR

Friendly, practical training that gives you real-world skills, not just a certificate.

Our Story

Sydney First Aid & CPR was created to make first aid training fast, practical and enjoyable. With a healthcare background and years of teaching experience, our trainers understand both the clinical guidelines and the reality of emergencies at home, work and in the community.


We keep our classes small so you get plenty of hands-on practice with manikins and equipment. Our aim is for every student to walk away feeling confident, not nervous, about using their skills.

Why choose us?

Experienced trainers

Courses are delivered by trainers with real clinical and teaching experience, so you get practical tips, not just theory.

Flexible delivery

Blended learning options with online theory and short face-to-face sessions, perfect for busy workplaces and individuals.

Workplace & group training

We can come to your workplace or community group for private sessions across Sydney. Ask us about group discounts.
