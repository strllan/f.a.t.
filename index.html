First Aid and CPR Courses Sydney

Nationally recognised first aid and CPR training with same-day certificates. Perfect for workplaces, students and community members.

Why train with Sydney First Aid & CPR?

We offer small classes, friendly trainers and practical, hands-on skills that give you the confidence to respond in an emergency. Our courses are aligned with Australian Resuscitation Council guidelines and suitable for individuals, workplaces and community groups.

Popular Courses

HLTAID011

Provide First Aid

The full first aid qualification covering common injuries, medical emergencies, burns, bleeding, shock and more. Suitable for most workplaces.

HLTAID009

Provide Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)

Short CPR-only course covering DRSABCD, chest compressions, rescue breaths and AED use. Ideal for annual refresher training.

CHILDCARE

Childcare First Aid

First aid for educators and carers, with a focus on babies and children, asthma, anaphylaxis and common childhood emergencies.
