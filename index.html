Community First Aid and CPR Courses Sydney

Registered first aid and CPR training. Perfect for certificates for workplaces, students and community members.

Why train with Community First Aid Training?

We offer small classes, friendly trainers and practical, hands-on skills that gives you the confidence to respond in an emergency. Our courses are aligned with Australian Resuscitation Council guidelines and suitable for individuals, workplaces and community groups.

Popular Courses

HLTAID011

Provide First Aid

The full first aid qualification covering common injuries, medical emergencies, burns, bleeding, shock and more. Suitable for most workplaces.

HLTAID009

Provide Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)

Short CPR-only course covering DRSABCD, chest compressions, rescue breaths and AED use. Ideal for annual refresher training.

HLTAID012

First Aid In An Education & Care Setting

Short, childcare-focused first aid course covering DRSABCD, asthma, anaphylaxis, bleeding, choking and responding to common injuries in an education & care setting.
